**Effective Date / 生效日期 / Date d'effet**: 2026-02-22

## English

### 1. Overview
GuniaorTax is a local‑first bookkeeping app. We do not operate servers to collect your personal data.

### 2. Data We Collect
- **On‑device data**: transactions, categories, receipts, and lexicon data are stored locally on your device.
- **Voice input**: speech recognition is performed using Apple’s Speech framework. Audio may be processed by Apple according to their policies. We do not store or upload your audio to our own servers.
- **Photos**: receipt images are stored locally on your device.

### 3. How We Use Data
We use data only to provide app functionality: recording, categorizing, reporting, and exporting your bookkeeping data.

### 4. Sharing
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. If you export or share a report, that action is initiated by you.

### 5. Security
Data is stored locally. Optional backups can be exported and encrypted with a password you choose.

### 6. Your Choices
You can delete the app to remove all local data. You control any exports and shares.
